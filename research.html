Research

I'm a physicist and data scientist with a Ph.D. specializing in theoretical physics. I integrate rigorous science with practical innovation to push boundaries in physics and AI. Let’s explore the universe, one discovery at a time! My work bridges cutting-edge research and real-world applications in diverse fields:

Warp Drive Theory

My primary focus in general relativity! I explore faster-than-light propulsion, solving Einstein’s equations to model spacetime geometries with exotic matter.

Black Holes & Wormholes

I study their stability, thermodynamics, and connections to quantum gravity, uncovering the mysteries of these exotic spacetime phenomena.

Econophysics

Applying physics principles to model market behaviors, wealth distributions, and critical phenomena in economic systems.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Designing and deploying machine learning solutions in industries like healthcare, banking, and entertainment. I specialize in scalable AI systems and anomaly detection.

Quantum Computing

Exploring hybrid quantum-classical algorithms and their potential to solve computationally intractable problems.

Longevity Science

Leveraging AI to analyze biological data, predict aging biomarkers, and optimize interventions to extend lifespan and healthspan.

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)

Pioneering research on the foundations of AGI, tackling neural network scalability, emergent behaviors, and computational efficiency.
