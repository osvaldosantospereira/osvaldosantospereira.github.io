Hi, I'm Oz.

I am a theoretical physicist and data scientist. I work with pragmatic machine learning applications on the private market and research on classical gravitation (warp drive theory) in academia. I am the founder and CDO of sciencedata.ai, a startup which I like to brand as a SciTech, a startup with science based approach (I hope people quote me on this brand in the future).

ScienceData has a scientific approach to solve technological problems. It has a branch on Research and Development (R&D) with research on machine learning algorithms on several branches of the private market, such as industry, hospitals, ecommerce, retail, banking and so on and so forth. ScienceData also provide learning materials, technical training on machine learning and data science for companies that are going under digital and data driven transformation.

I hope you enjoy the material you can find here in my personal website and feel free to poke around and contact me.
